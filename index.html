Who am I?

Hi, I'm Ross, a Frontend JavaScript Developer from Norfolk, England. I love art, design and tech, especially when I am able to break something, see how it works, and put it back together again. Besides English, I speak French and a little bit of Spanish. I'm passionate about learning, having been an English teacher for 7 years, which means I am always looking for new challenges to smash and skills to obtain. I love the work I do, and I am always up for new projects. If you would like some help with yours, shoot me a message!
